Library

Showcasing creativity and scholarship

The library has been built to the Standards prescribed by the AICTE. It contributes to the fulfillment of the College’s mission by selecting, acquiring, organizing, maintaining and making accessible a collection of printed and non-printed, primary and secondary materials that will support the educational and research aspirations of both students and the faculty .Specialized collections of books, journals and non-book materials are available in Humanities, Basic Sciences, Engineering and Technology.
