College Profile

Shreenivasa Engineering College (SEC) is a dynamic community of scholars and learners situated on a picturesque location near Dharmapuri, Tamil Nadu. Shreenivasa Engineering College (SEC) is an innovative and vibrant place of learning, where undergraduates and graduate students pursue their educational and research interests in order to lead the next generation in transforming the engineering discipline.

Shreenivasa Engineering College (SEC) opens its doors to all who work hard and dream big. Through its teaching, research and public service, Shreenivasa Engineering College (SEC) is driving the local economy and serving the local community. Working across disciplines is a core component of our approach to education. The college offers Premium quality learning spaces, resources and technologies.

Shreenivasa Engineering College (SEC) seeks to achieve the highest levels of distinction in the discovery and transmission of knowledge and understanding.

Vision

To offer the highest quality technical education to the brightest engineering students in a dynamic environment of mutual learning and to transform them into confident, effective, respected and socially responsible engineers prepared to face the toughest challenges.

Mission

To Prepare the rural youth as effective and responsible engineers for global requirements by providing quality education.

To Evolve into globally recognized Institutions in the frontier areas of Engineering and Technology.

To Respond effectively to the needs of the industry and changing world.

Quality Policy

To Provide value based quality education for developing the student as a competent and responsible Citizen.

To Contribute to the nation and beyond through the State-Of-The-Art Technology.

To Continuously improve our services.
