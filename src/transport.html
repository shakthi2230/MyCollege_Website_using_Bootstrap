College Transport Facilities

1. Salem(Pappireddipatti, Ayodhyapattinam)

2. Tharamangalam(Denishpet, Theevattipatti, Omalur)

3. Vellar(Mutthampatti, Thoppur)

4. Palayampudhur(Kadathur, Dharmapuri, nallampalli)

7. Kaveripattinam(Dharmapuri, Karimangalam)
